Case #402: The Midnight Heist

SOLVED

Mumbai, 2023 | Property Crime

A high-profile burglary occurred due to social media oversharing. The victims posted real-time vacation photos, alerting organized gangs of their absence.

Lesson Learned:

Avoid posting real-time location updates on public social media profiles.

Case #899: The 'KYC' Trap

AWARENESS

Bangalore, 2024 | Cyber Crime

Victim received a message stating "Bank Account Blocked". They downloaded a screen-sharing app as instructed by the fake support agent, losing ₹5 Lakhs.

Lesson Learned:

Banks NEVER ask you to install screen-sharing apps like AnyDesk or TeamViewer for KYC.

Cyber Crime

Immediate Actions If Crime Occurs:

  • 1. Prioritize Safety: Move to a secure location immediately.
  • 2. Medical Aid: If injured, go to the nearest hospital. Doctors legally must treat emergency cases (Medico-Legal).
  • 3. Preserve Evidence: Do not wash clothes (in sexual assault cases) or touch the crime scene.
  • 4. Dial 112: Report the incident to get an official log.
