POCSO &
CHILD SAFETY

The Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act 2012 provides a robust legal framework to protect minors from exploitation and abuse.

GROOMING TACTICS

Trust Building

Gradual isolation of a child from their parents through gifts, special attention, and "secrets".

Digital Exploitation

Coercing minors into sharing sensitive images via online gaming or social platforms.
