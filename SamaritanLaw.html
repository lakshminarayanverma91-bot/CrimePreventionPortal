THE GOOD SAMARITAN LAW

Many citizens hesitate to help crime or accident victims due to fear of police harassment or legal entanglements. India's Good Samaritan Law protects you.

No Mandatory Disclosure

You are not forced to reveal your name or personal details when helping a victim.

Protection from Liability

You cannot be held civilly or criminally liable for accidental injury caused while trying to help someone in good faith.
