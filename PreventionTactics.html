Prevention Tactics

"The best defense is not being there."

Street Awareness

  • The 20-Foot Rule: Be aware of anyone entering your 20-foot radius in secluded areas.
  • Digital Distraction: Avoid using headphones while walking alone at night.
  • Vocalize: If followed, shout "FIRE" instead of "HELP" – it attracts more immediate attention.

Residential Safety

  • Verification: Never open doors for 'service personnel' without verifying ID through a peephole.
  • Smart Lighting: Use motion-sensor lights in driveways and balconies.
  • Police Verification: Always conduct police verification for domestic help or tenants.
