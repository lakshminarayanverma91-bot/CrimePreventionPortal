CRIMES AGAINST
WOMEN

Encompasses domestic violence, harassment, stalking, and systemic gender-based exploitation. Legal literacy is the strongest defense.

EXECUTION PATTERNS

Domestic Control

Systematic isolation from financial resources and social support to maintain dominance.

Stalking

Both physical and digital persistence intended to cause fear and psychological distress.

WARNING SIGNS

  • Sudden unexplained withdrawal from social gatherings.
